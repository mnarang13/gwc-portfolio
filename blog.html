My First Day at Girls Who Code

July 20, 2020

Today was my first day at the GWC Virtual Summer Immersion Program. We reviewed an overview of the program content and guidelines, then did a sisterhood activity, where we drew stick figures and added features according to our random preferences. Lastly, we began learning HTML, and went over the instructions for creating this website. I had a lot of fun meeting everyone, and it seems like an amazing group - I'm super excited for the next two weeks!
